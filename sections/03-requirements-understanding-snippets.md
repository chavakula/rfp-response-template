## Snippet: Business Context Paragraph Templates

### Cloud Transformation
[CLIENT NAME] operates in the [INDUSTRY] sector, where [MARKET DRIVER — e.g., "digital health, connected devices, real-time analytics"] is transforming how organizations [DELIVER VALUE]. Their current platform [CURRENT STATE DESCRIPTION] faces challenges with [CHALLENGES — e.g., "scalability, compliance, legacy technology debt, operational cost"]. This RFP represents a strategic initiative to [OBJECTIVE — e.g., "modernize the technology stack, achieve global scale, and meet evolving regulatory requirements"].

### Managed Services Takeover
[CLIENT NAME]'s existing [PLATFORM/APPLICATION] has reached a maturity stage where the focus shifts from feature development to operational excellence, performance optimization, and continuous compliance. The RFP seeks a partner capable of [OBJECTIVES — e.g., "24/7 support, proactive monitoring, cost optimization, and incremental enhancements"] while maintaining [SLA COMMITMENTS].

### Greenfield Build
[CLIENT NAME] has identified a market opportunity in [DOMAIN] and requires a new [PLATFORM/PRODUCT] built from the ground up. The solution must [KEY REQUIREMENTS — e.g., "handle X users/devices, comply with Y regulations, integrate with Z systems"] and be delivered within [TIMELINE].
