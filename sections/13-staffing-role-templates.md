## Onshore / Offshore Mix Guidelines

| Engagement Type | Recommended Mix | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Regulated (Healthcare, Finance, Gov) | 40–60% Onshore | Compliance, data access, client proximity |
| Enterprise SaaS | 30–40% Onshore | Architecture oversight + nearshore delivery |
| Product Engineering | 20–30% Onshore | Deep dev work offshore, arch + security onshore |
| Managed Services | 10–20% Onshore | L1/L2 offshore, L3 + client liaison onshore |
